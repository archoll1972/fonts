On the right size, an Oshawa postcard from 2013. It was taken during the Canada Day celebrations from July 1.

I've always enjoyed matching the wonderment of fireworks' exploding energy with the preciseness of long exposure photography at nightime. I think this image encapsulates both, the serenity of water and open sky and the sudden burst of light that shows all our surroundings. I believe this description defines Canadian values.
